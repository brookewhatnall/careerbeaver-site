Getting started

Just want to look around?

Open CareerBeaver and choose “Explore with demo data” on the welcome screen. This loads sample jobs, drafts, sent applications and statistics so you can try every feature immediately — no account, API key, or email setup required. You can turn it on or off any time under Settings ▸ Demo Mode.

Setting it up for real

  • AI key: add a free Groq key (or your own Anthropic, OpenAI, or Gemini key) in Settings to generate cover emails. Keys are stored in your macOS Keychain.
  • CVs: point the app at a folder of your CVs — one per kind of work.
  • Email: connect Gmail, iCloud, or any SMTP account to send applications. Most providers need an app-specific password.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use CareerBeaver?

No. CareerBeaver has no login. Everything runs on your Mac and your data stays on your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your listings, drafts, applications and settings are stored locally on your Mac. API keys and email passwords are kept in the macOS Keychain. Text is only sent to the AI provider you choose (to generate drafts) and to your email provider (to send applications).

The AI isn't generating emails.

Make sure you've added an API key under Settings ▸ AI Provider and that your Mac is online. The free Groq option needs only a free key (no credit card). You can also try Demo Mode to see how generation works without any key.

My emails won't send.

Email sending uses your own SMTP account. Gmail and iCloud require an app-specific password rather than your normal password — see the step-by-step guidance in Settings ▸ Email Sending, then use “Send test email to myself” to confirm.

How do I delete my data?

Open Settings ▸ Storage ▸ Reveal in Finder and delete the data folder, or remove the app — no data is held on any server.

Which macOS version do I need?

CareerBeaver requires macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later.
